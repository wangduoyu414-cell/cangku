# federated-incident-governor brief（技能简报）

## Goal（目标）
- Generate a reusable federated incident-governor skill from long, messy, multilingual, policy-heavy inputs with contradictory constraints.

## Scope（边界）
- Convert federated incident-governor requests into a reusable, bounded skill.
- Preserve governance rules, escalation boundaries, multilingual evidence, and baseline expectations.
- Bind every major action to evidence and follow-up validation.

## Non-Scope（非边界）
- Do not execute incident remediation.
- Do not replace legal, security, or executive approval.
- Do not broaden into organization-wide architecture design.

## Inputs（输入）
- messy incident narratives（杂乱事件叙述）
- contradictory policy notes（矛盾策略注记）
- cross-team failure clusters（跨团队失败簇）

## Outputs（输出）
- governor skill draft（治理技能草案）
- baseline comparison（基线对比）
- iteration backlog（迭代待办）
- experience ledger（经验账本）

## Risks（风险）
- Flatten contradictory constraints into a false single rule.
- Lose escalation nuance under multilingual compression.
- Over-trigger on plain policy summaries.

## Success Bar（成功门槛）
- Must keep contradictory constraints visible.
- Must produce semantically filled artifacts with no template residue.
- Must retain governance non-scope clearly.
